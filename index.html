about me

LinkedIn GitHub

I’m a back-end software developer with a background in writing, editing, and music.

In my previous work writing in corporate, news, and small business settings, I learned first-hand that even small improvements in website design can make a huge difference in productivity and user experience. That’s why I decided to study Java at Grand Circus — I wanted to combine my communication skills with the ability to design software to make communication easier and more effective. I’m looking forward to finding a role at the intersection of tech and the humanities where I can make the most of the skills at my disposal.

Outside of business hours, I keep busy as a freelance arts writer — currently with the Michigan Quarterly Review — and as a producer and multi-instrumentalist playing with several artists in the Detroit area.
