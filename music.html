MUSIC

I've been a musician almost my entire life, and I still try to get as many opportunities to play and write as I can. I started studying piano and violin when I was about 8 years old, but I got my first guitar when I was 12 and fell in love with it right away. Since then, the guitar has been my primary instrument (with an honorable mention to the electric bass). My go-tos these days are a 1999 MIM Fender Stratocaster and a 1996 Taylor 410.

In the last few years, I've had the chance to work with a bunch of incredible artists and perform at venues around the Detroit area. Songs I've helped produce have been written up in the Detroit Metro Times, played on WDET's "Culture Shift", performed to a sold-out crowd at St. Andrew's Hall in Detroit, and even appeared in an episode of the CW's "Batwoman."
