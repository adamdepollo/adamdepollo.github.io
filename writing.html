WRITING

I've been writing professionally since 2012, when I got my first gig covering concerts and writing about jazz for the Michigan Daily in Ann Arbor, MI. Since then, I've been published internationally in media ranging from newspapers, fine arts journals, and magazines to whitepapers, policy documents, and PR releases.
